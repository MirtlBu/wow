Everlasting Summer

It is a visual novel, produced by Soviet Games, that tells the story of a 25-year-old man living in modern Russia who finds himself mysteriously transported to a summer camp somewhere in the former Soviet Union.
Interface
The game uses texts and sprites in a manner similar to many Japanese visual novels.
Development
Game development started in May 2008 based off an image posted to the Russian IIchan imageboard.
Feedback
Two Russian-language gaming sites, itndaily and Канобу, gave it generally positive reviews.

Characters in game

Semyon, a male of age 25 in the opening but regressed to 18 during most of the game, is the protagonist of the game. He is portrayed as having brown hair.
Slavya
Slavya has gold hair in two braids that go down to her waist, is a nature-lover, and is the "good girl".
Lena
Lena, who has short purple hair, is introverted and can often be seen reading a book.
Alisa
Alisa has short orange hair and is the more rebellious "bad girl", who also plays guitar.
Ulyana
Ulyana is about 3-4 years younger than the other girls and has medium-length red hair.
Miku
Miku is Japanese and has long cyan hair in two ponytails. She can often be found in the music room.
Yulya
Yulya is a catgirl with brown hair. She is only seen in some of the later unlocked storylines.
